Medicine

Undergraduate Interview preparation for any Australian University

  • Preparatory Phase: 8+ modules
  • Practice Phase: up to 3 one-on-one mock interviews

Interview Preparation program is specifically designed to help you ace your interviews for any Australian University. With 8+ modules, our program covers everything you need to know for your interview.

Each module includes a pre-recorded lecture that covers important content such as traits and strategies, a 1.5 hour tutorial, self-directed practice on Discord for 7 hours a week, ethical practice questions on Notion, and a 48 hour response time for any questions or concerns.

In the practice phase, your skills will already be well-developed. We offer up to 3 one-on-one mock interviews, each lasting 1.5 hours. These sessions include a 45-minute, no-break simulation of your desired university's interview, followed by 45 minutes of feedback tailored to your specific performance.

Application Writing

Available for purchase separately for non-students

  • JCU Application Considerations
  • UNSW Application Considerations
  • WSU Community Station Considerations

Application Writing program offers application writing services for James Cook University (JCU), University of New South Wales (UNSW), and Western Sydney University Community Station (WSU) considerations. These services are included in the program for students, but can also be purchased separately for non-students.

In addition, Application Writing program includes mock interviews, which consist of 1.5 hour sessions. The mock interviews are divided into 45 minutes of MMI (Multiple Mini Interviews), semi-structured medical/dental interviews, and 45 minutes of one-on-one feedback with a trained interviewer. We allow you to emulate any university of your choice for your mock interview. Our program also provides marking criteria for every question asked during the mock interviews, to help you understand where you excel and where you need improvement.
